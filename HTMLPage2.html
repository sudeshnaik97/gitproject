FAQ's

Q) What is an EMI ?

EMI (Equated Monthly Installment) is the amount payable to the lending institution every month, till the loan is completely paid off. It comprises of the interest as well as the principal amount.

Q) How can I know my Home Loan Eligibility?

Your age should not be more than 65 years.
Go to eligibility calculator tab to know about your eligibilty.

Q) What is the process of getting a home loan?

Once you match the eligibility criteria and fill up the related formalities,you will get an appointment date.

Q) Can I close my home loan? What are the charges for prepaying my loan?

Nil for Home Loans and Home Improvement Loans with floating rate of interest.

Q) Do I require a guarantor to get a home loan?

Welcome-Home doesn’t ask for a guarantor,while some other financial institutions do ask for guarantors.

Q) What is the time required for home loan application approval?

About 3-15 days, subject to proper documentation provided by the home loan applicant.

Q) What is the maximum amount I can borrow for a home loan?
